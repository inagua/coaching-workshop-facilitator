# Facilitator Notes

Things to remember:

- Focus on systems, not people.
- Assume positive intent.
- Encourage balanced participation.
- Challenge ideas, not individuals.
- Create psychological safety.
- Make outcomes actionable.
- Leave with clear ownership.
- Document decisions.
